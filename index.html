The Lion and the Cat

Once in the heart of the savanna, a mighty lion rested beneath the shade of an acacia tree.

A small stray cat approached with no fear, curious and clever.

The lion growled, but the cat spoke, “I may be small, but I carry wisdom the jungle forgets.”

Intrigued, the lion listened. They spoke for hours — about hunting, patience, and survival.

From that day forward, the lion and the cat met every week, exchanging tales. Strength met strategy.

In the wild, sometimes wisdom walks on tiny paws.
